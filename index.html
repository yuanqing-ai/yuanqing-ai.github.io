  • AIRPOSE Dataset

    • We have created a dataset AIRPOSE used for aircraft pose estimation, which includes 200k synthetic images and 10k real images with annotations of bounding boxs and keypoints. There are in total 12 keypoints defined for aircrafts. The sythetic images were generated using 4 types of aircafts(Boeing 787, 747; Airbus 320, 330) in total 15 different liveries under 18 different camera positions and 7 different climate conditions (clear skies, scattered cloud, broken cloud, over cast, rain, snow, storm) in 115 different airports with various ligthing conditions. All of the 3D aircrafts are modeled realisticly according to the real world aircrafts and each airport is also modeled based on the corresponding real world airport. We used RTX 3070Ti for high quality rendering and obtained synthetic images of size 1920x1080.
